Shipping Policy

Last updated: 10 Jun 2026

We aim to process and dispatch orders as quickly as possible after payment has been successfully received.

This Shipping Policy explains how orders are processed, shipped, and delivered.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. During busy periods, holidays, or promotional events, processing times may be slightly longer.

Orders are not processed or shipped on weekends or public holidays.

Shipping Destinations

We ship to customers in many countries worldwide. Shipping availability and delivery times may vary depending on the destination.

If we are unable to ship to your location, you will be notified during checkout or shortly after placing your order.

Estimated Delivery Times

Delivery times depend on your location, shipping carrier, customs procedures, and other factors outside our control.

  • United Kingdom: typically 2–7 business days
  • European Union: typically 5–14 business days
  • International destinations: typically 7–21 business days

These delivery estimates are not guaranteed and should be considered approximate.

Tracking Information

Where available, tracking information will be provided once your order has been dispatched.

Please allow up to 48 hours for tracking information to become active after shipment.

Customs, Duties, and Taxes

For international orders, customs duties, import taxes, VAT, and other fees may be charged by your local customs authority.

These charges are the responsibility of the customer unless otherwise stated at checkout.

We are not responsible for delays caused by customs inspections or import procedures.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are displayed during checkout before payment is completed.

Where free shipping is offered, any applicable conditions will be clearly displayed on the website.

Incorrect Shipping Address

Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information.

If you need to change your shipping address, please contact us as soon as possible at info@pampoprint.com .

Once an order has been shipped, we may be unable to modify the delivery address.

Lost, Delayed, or Missing Shipments

If your order has not arrived within the expected delivery period, please contact us and we will investigate the issue with the shipping carrier.

While we will do our best to assist, we are not responsible for delays caused by circumstances beyond our control, including customs processing, weather conditions, transportation disruptions, or carrier-related issues.

Order Cancellations

If you wish to cancel an order, please contact us as soon as possible at info@pampoprint.com .

Orders that have already been processed or shipped may not be eligible for cancellation and may instead be subject to our Refund Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about shipping, delivery, or order status, please contact us:
