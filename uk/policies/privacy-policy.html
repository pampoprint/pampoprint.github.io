Privacy Policy

When you visit our Site, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address

We use personal information for the following purposes:

Payments

We use third-party payment processors to process payments securely.

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features.

We may share personal information with:
